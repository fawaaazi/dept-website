Our Academic Programs

Discover the comprehensive undergraduate and postgraduate programs we offer to shape the future of technology.

B.Tech in Information Technology

Our Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program in Information Technology is a comprehensive four-year undergraduate course designed to provide students with a robust foundation in computer science, software development, and networking. The curriculum is crafted to meet the evolving demands of the IT industry, blending theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical experience.

  • Strong foundation in programming, algorithms, and data structures.
  • In-depth knowledge of database management and web technologies.
  • Specialized training in areas like artificial intelligence and cybersecurity.
  • Emphasis on project-based learning and industry internships.

M.Tech in Network Engineering

The Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Network Engineering is a two-year postgraduate program aimed at professionals and graduates seeking to specialize in the field of computer networks. This program covers advanced topics in network design, management, and security, preparing students to tackle complex challenges in modern networking infrastructure.

  • Advanced understanding of network protocols and architecture.
  • Hands-on experience with modern network simulation tools.
  • Focus on network security, wireless networks, and cloud computing.
  • Opportunities for cutting-edge research and dissertation work.
